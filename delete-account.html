What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your booking history
  • Your account credentials
  • Any saved preferences

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

The fastest way to delete your account:

  1. Open the Courtak app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Scroll down to Danger Zone
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision
Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to our support team:

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Your personal data is permanently deleted from our servers
  • We may retain anonymized booking statistics for business analytics
  • Court owners may retain booking records for their own business purposes
