Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our court booking service:

  • Account information: Name, phone number, and email address when you register
  • Booking data: Your booking history including courts, dates, and times
  • Authentication data: Encrypted password for account security

We do not collect location data, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the booking service:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and display your court bookings
  • Allow venues to confirm your reservations
  • Provide customer support when you contact us

Data Sharing

We share limited information only when necessary:

  • With venues: Your name and phone number are shared with court venues to fulfill your booking
  • Service providers: We use secure hosting services to store your data

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

We protect your data using industry-standard security measures:

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View your personal information in the app's Profile section
  • Update: Edit your name and email at any time
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all data from Profile > Danger Zone > Delete Account

For any data requests, contact us at support@courtak.com.

Children's Privacy

Courtak is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately at support@courtak.com and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app. Continued use of Courtak after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@courtak.com.
